online calculus resources

  • Sage Notebook (open source alternative to Maple, Mathematica, and Matlab; runs in a web browser)
  • Lurch (open source mathematics validation software)
  • Stewart Calculus (website for the calculus book that I am currently teaching out of)
  • Wolfram|Alpha (a computational knowledge engine)
  • MIT Mathematics OpenCourseWare (free course material and videos for mathematics courses, offerred through the MIT OpenCourseWare Project, also available through iTunesU)
  • Ever wonder, "When will I use math?" If so, check out http://www.whenwilliusemath.com/.
  • Visual Calculus (a cool website with Flash tutorials covering a variety of topics in calculus)
  • Paul's Online Math Notes (an excellent website by Paul Dawkins at Lamar University)
  • Calculus.org (a plethora of resources for the calculus student)
  • trig unit circle (PDF file)
