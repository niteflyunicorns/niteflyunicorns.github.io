announcements

  • Class begins on Monday, July 13. We meet in Hyde 318.
  • Throughout the course, we will be using software called Group Explorer, which compliments our textbook Visual Group Theory. The software is free and works on all operating systems. Feel free to download and install the software on your own computer. The textbook will be available in the bookstore, but you can also order it online from the MAA Bookstore. If you become a member of the MAA, you can get a discount on the book.
